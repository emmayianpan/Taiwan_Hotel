Hotel Map
The map shows the location of listed hotels in Taiwan provided from Tourism Bureau. There are three hotels located outside of main island of Taiwan including two hotels in Penghu and one hotel in Kinmen. The data is collected by December, 2020 (126 hotels in total and 28,780 of total room counts) and is not included Motel or other types of accommodations.
